# Courier Contracts Manual

Want to make a living on the open road? Become a Courier!

## 1. What is a Courier Contract?
Towns often need to send goods to each other but lack the infrastructure to do it automatically. That's where you come in. Even automated towns need a human touch for long-distance logistics.

A **Courier Contract** is a job offer:
-   **Pickup**: The Source Town.
-   **Destination**: The Target Town.
-   **Cargo**: A specific set of items.
-   **Reward**: The payment you receive upon successful delivery.

## 2. How to Accept a Job
1.  Go to a **Contract Board** (or Town Interface).
2.  Look for contracts marked as **Courier** or **Delivery**.
3.  Click "Accept".
4.  **Receive Cargo**: The items will be given to you (ensure you have inventory space!). Ideally, they are provided in a **Bundle** or **Crate** (depending on version).

## 3. Making the Delivery
1.  Travel to the **Destination Town**.
2.  Find the **Trade Interface** block or the drop-off point.
3.  Interact with the interface to **Deliver** the items.
4.  **Validation**: The system checks if you delivered the correct items and quantity.

## 4. Payment
-   Once the delivery is confirmed, the **Reward** is automatically transferred to your personal account (or inventory).
-   **Reputation**: Successful deliveries may increase your standing with both towns.

## 5. Risks
-   **Time Limits**: Some contracts have deadlines. If you're late, you might fail the contract and face a penalty.
-   **Loss of Cargo**: If you die or lose the items, you cannot complete the contract. You may be fined the value of the lost goods!

## 6. Pro Tips
-   **Route Planning**: Accept multiple contracts going to the same area to maximize efficiency.
-   **Vehicles**: Use Horses, Boats, or Trains (if available) to speed up travel.
-   **Infrastructure**: Build roads between your towns to make courier runs faster and safer.
